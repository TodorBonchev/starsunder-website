About the Game

Starsunder is a 2D top-down multiplayer spaceship arena game built in Unity 6 with Photon Fusion 2 for cross-play between PC and mobile. Battle 2–8 players in an inertia-based arena, master unique ship archetypes, and unleash special abilities to dominate the match.
